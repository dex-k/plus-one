Help

To get started, click the "I'm ready" button on the home page. Then fill out the form with your name, and the type and amount of questions you want.

At any time you can go to a different part of the app by clicking the medu button in the header. You won't lose your progress unless you begin another game by filling in the form again.

  1. Addition

    Addition is calculating the total of two different numbers. Addiding one number to another gives you a result that is the sum of both numbers.

  2. Subtraction

    Subtraction is finding the difference between two numbers.

  3. Multiplication

    Mulitiplication is just repeated addition. It is finding the result of adding the number to itsself an amount of times.

  4. Division

    Division is just repeated subtraction. It is finding how many times you can take one number away from another number before there is nothing left.

PLUS ONE

Welcome to Plus One!


Plus One is an interactive online maths trainer, which allows you to practice addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.

When you're ready, you can pick what type of questions you want to practice, and how many.
Addition and subtraction questions will range 1-100, and multiplication and division questions will range from 1-12.
Make sure to be quick, because speedy answers will give you extra points!
